About The Magic Bean

The Magic Bean delivers unforgettable magic shows designed especially for kids, families, schools, libraries, and community events. Packed with laughter, surprises, and audience participation, every show keeps kids amazed from beginning to end.

From birthday parties to stage performances, The Magic Bean brings a colorful mix of comedy magic, visual illusions, and interactive tricks that make every child feel like part of the show.

Perfect for ages 1 to 101, and fun for the whole family.
